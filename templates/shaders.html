Realtime VFX

Fire Shader
Shader that pans a gradated wave texture with alpha erosion over the top. You can also add noise distortion and world position offset to make it more realistic. It has U/V panning options for all the textures and you can tile them as well.
Fire Softness
Fire distortion
Trail Shader
Shader that pans a trail texture. You can adjust the length of the trail and how soft or hard it is. It also has U/V panning controls.
